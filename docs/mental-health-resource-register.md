## Governance

- Confirm organization, service, audience, number, hours, coverage, and disclosure from the official source.
- Remove or hide an entry if verification fails.
- Review the register on schedule and before every public release.
- Do not add crowdsourced or unverified crisis numbers.
- Resource calls, texts, and websites require a user confirmation.
- OPAi does not record resource use, call history, or selections.
- The directory does not diagnose, treat, counsel, assess risk, or contact emergency services automatically.
